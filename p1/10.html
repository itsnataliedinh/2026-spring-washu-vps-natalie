Korean Radish Stew

Big chunks of Korean radish, carrots, onions, shallot, potato, mushrooms, garlic and more make this a one-of-a-kind meal. The broth is velvety smooth and features a homemade deonjang paste and gochugaru from the smoky mountains of Gwangju. I highly recommend adding your favorite Tiny Chef protein and a sunny egg to your stew for even more flavor! This delicious bowl of stew will come with a side of rice and gets topped with scallions and toasted sesame seeds.

Yukgaejang +

Yukgaejang 육개장 is a spicy Korean soup chock full of ingredients, including Korean root vegetables and Korean sweet potato noodles. But don't worry, I didn't make it very spicy at all!


Korean gosari (a delicious Korean root vegetable), lotus root, Korean sweet potato, onion, carrot, mung bean sprouts, a wide variety of delicious mushrooms, and more are just a part of this epic stew! It's usually made with beef and beef stock, but since I try and make everything from the start vegan friendly, this version has zero animal products. It's also gluten-free, even with the noodles!

I HIGHLY RECOMMEND adding protein, a side of rice, and a sunny egg because that will just add to the many levels of flavor. You do not want to miss this special!!
