Final Deliverable

Restaurant Branding + Full Website


Tiny Chef is a resturant that only has two regular dishes. However it has a rotating menu of "weekly specials." Tiny Chef currently does not have any websites and relies on social media. This project scope includes designing both an identity system (logo & brand) and website to capture the ephemeral nature of Tiny Chef's menu. In addition, to a "Menu," there's also an "Archive."

There is a total of 5 pages including Home, Menu, Archive, About, & Contact

  • View the complete project: Tiny Chef STL
  • Research & Process: View the Figma mockups.

Takeaways

While I'm more comfortable mocking up designs in Figma, I challenged myself to develop a full fuctioning website. I utilized animations on the hero section and "weekly special" part of the home page. I also focused on micro-interactions, such as the interactive clock button to view the hours and the jukebox button that plays rock music. For the home page, I wanted to create an immersive experience that captured the interioir vibe and playful personality of the restaurant.

For the Menu/ Archive section, I decided to create the Archive page as a modal layover rather than be its own distinct page. I wanted to mimic a suprise reveal of coming across the blog as you would dig through a file cabinet before finding the folder. There's opportunity to refine the visual layout and micro typography, so it feels less cluttered. After the inital exercises, I was less focus on iteration and prioritized an overall system design.
